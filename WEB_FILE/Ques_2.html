My Hobby

Listening to music is one of my favorite hobbies. It helps me relax and stay focused, especially after a long day of college and assignments. I enjoy a wide variety of genres including classical, lo-fi, and pop.

Music creates a soothing environment in my hostel room. While lying on my bed, I often put on my headphones and drift into the melodies. It's my way of finding peace and solitude in the midst of a busy routine.
